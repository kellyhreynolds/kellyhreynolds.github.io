About Me

I'm a math tutor and I love what I do! I've been a middle school math teacher for the last seven years and I'm certified 6-12. I am taking this year off from teaching to spend time with my new baby, Delton. I miss the classroom but love staying home with my boy! I'll work Delton's naptimes around tutoring so that I can give my students my full attention.


During my years teaching I often tutored students before and after school. As a tutor, my goal is to raise students' confidence in math so that they are able to reason through the material on their own. I collaborate with the student and their classroom teacher to provide as much support as I can. I care about my students and want to see them succeed!
